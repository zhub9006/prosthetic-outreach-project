# Meeting Plan: Multi-City Outreach Coordinator Gathering

## Proposed Meeting Location: **Nashville, TN**

### Why Nashville?
- Geographic centroid of our three cities (~4-5 hour flight from each)
- Major airport (BNA) with direct flights from all three
- Affordable hotels and meeting venues
- Rich culinary and cultural scene for team bonding
