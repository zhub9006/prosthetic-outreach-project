# Clinical Trials for Affordable Prosthetic Solutions

Based on the **Prosthetic Access Atlas** analysis of **2,162** prosthetic-related clinical trials from ClinicalTrials.gov.

|--------|-------|
| Total registered trials | 2,162 |
| Recruiting trials | ~378 (17.4%) |
| Completed trials | ~923 (42.7%) |

## Innovative Solutions Identified
- 3D-Printed Prosthetics (LIMBER UniLeg, open-source)
- Neural-Controlled Prostheses (e-OPRA, EMG-controlled)
- Adaptive Prosthetic Foot (VSPA Foot - variable stiffness)
- Telerehabilitation (remote rehab programs)
- Wireless EMG Control (ASTERISK System)
